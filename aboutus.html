Welcome to GoForEvent

    Goforevent is a platform where you can host your event for online ticket booking. Also helps the organizer in promoting the event for the right audience.

    Goforevent is your one platform to help you build, manage, and grow your college events. We make your job of planning and hosting your college events by easing the hassle of creating a platform for ticketing and featuring events where it can be visible. Not just that! we provide information and updates about workshops and events all over the country for curious students.
